Disciplinary Action

STATE OF OREGON

CENTRAL OREGON SUPREME CIRCUIT COURT

Case No. 20-SC-45673

In the Matter of Tyler Robert Lopez Perez

On the 27th day of August, 2020, it was ordered by this court that Tyler Robert Lopez Perez was found responsible for delegating a Certified Nursing Assistant (CNA) to administer medication without proper supervision, resulting in the patient receiving an incorrect dosage of morphine, leading to an overdose.

The patient was treated immediately with Naloxone HCL and stabilized within 30 minutes. The Court ordered that a written disciplinary statement be attached to the license of Tyler Robert Lopez Perez until the 25th day of August, 2025, as a formal notice of this disciplinary action. The CNA's certification was subsequently revoked as part of the court's order.
